About Vega LLC

Independent, nimble, and built for vital workflows.

Vega LLC is a high-tech startup organization headquartered in the New York City area. We are independently owned and built to accommodate a number of vertical industries where daily operations depend on reliable software.

Our roots are in back-office applications because that is where the health of an organization often becomes visible. As our founder says, "You can make as much money as you want, but if you cannot control where it goes, you will never succeed."

Vega focuses on quality, robust, user-friendly products that deliver long-term business value at prices that make modernization accessible.

01

Expense Management

ExpenseOnTheGo helps individuals, business owners, and corporations record, preserve, and submit expense transactions from mobile workflows. Personal and professional versions support a cleaner, more convenient way to digitize expense processes.

02

Payment Solutions

Vega developed a patent-pending approach for smartphone-based payments and money transfers across consumer and business use cases. The goal is a flexible, secure, low-cost, and reliable path for X2X payment transactions.
